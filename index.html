The Chef To Save The World

Hailey Sooknanan

Game Developer in the Making

Pitch

"In Chef to Save the World, you are a master chef on a quest to defeat hunger across three struggling kingdoms. Travel through vibrant lands, gather unique ingredients, and craft dishes that heal the starving nations..."

Theme - Outplay Hunger

This game touches on hunger in various countries. It shows the impact of hunger and how the user can combat this problem.

My Why

I have seen many places with families and people starving due to war and other concerns. So this game is to open the eyes of many.
